# Product Scope

This document defines what is included and excluded from the initial product scope.

---

## In Scope

User account creation

Campaign creation

Campaign discovery

Campaign participation

Payment processing

Campaign progress tracking

Order history

---

## Out of Scope (Initial Version)

Advanced analytics for influencers

Automated logistics optimization

Recommendation systems

Complex influencer management tools

---

## Target Users

Influencers launching product campaigns

Followers supporting influencer campaigns

Platform administrators
