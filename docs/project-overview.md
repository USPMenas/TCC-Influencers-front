# Project Overview

This platform enables influencers to launch product campaigns and validate demand before production.

Followers can join campaigns and pre-order products. If the campaign reaches the minimum required goal, the product is manufactured and delivered.

The platform connects three actors:

Influencers  
Launch campaigns and promote them to their audience.

Consumers  
Support campaigns by purchasing products.

Platform  
Provides infrastructure for campaign hosting, payments, and logistics coordination.

---

## Key Value Proposition

For Influencers

Ability to launch products without financial risk.

For Consumers

Access to exclusive influencer products.

For the Platform

Enable a creator-driven commerce ecosystem.

---

Campaign creation
