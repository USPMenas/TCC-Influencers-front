# TCC-Influencers-front

This project implements a platform where influencers can launch product campaigns and their audience can support them through pre-orders. The platform acts as an intermediary between influencers, consumers, and production logistics.

The core idea is simple:

Influencers launch campaigns for products they want to promote.  
Followers can join the campaign and pre-order the product.  
If the campaign reaches the minimum production goal, the product is manufactured and delivered.

This model allows creators to validate demand before production and reduces financial risk.

---

## Main Concepts

Campaign  
A campaign represents a product launch initiated by an influencer. It contains a product description, a funding goal, a deadline, and campaign updates.

Support / Order  
Users can support a campaign by purchasing the product during the campaign period.

Goal  
Each campaign has a minimum production goal. If the goal is not reached before the deadline, the campaign is cancelled.

Platform Role  
The platform manages payments, campaign visibility, order tracking, and campaign progress.

---

## Project Goals

Build a scalable and mobile-first platform that enables influencers to:

- Launch product campaigns
- Validate demand before manufacturing
- Engage their audience
- Track campaign performance
